Frequently Asked Questions

FQS

What services does Saint-Andre Medical Center offer?

We offer a wide range of services, including specialized care in Cardiology, Hepatology, Neurology, Eye Care, and Pediatrics. Our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology to provide accurate diagnoses and effective treatments for our patients..

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can easily schedule an appointment by visiting our Appointment Booking page on the website or by calling our clinic directly at [Clinic's Phone Number]. We also offer online booking for your convenience.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please contact us to verify if your specific provider is accepted. For uninsured patients, we offer affordable payment options and assistance.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any medical records or test results relevant to your condition. If you're seeing a specialist, having a referral from your primary care physician will help expedite the process.

How can I access my medical records?

You can request access to your medical records through our Patient Portal or by contacting our office directly. We ensure that your health information is handled with the utmost care and confidentiality.
